Participate & Collaborate

Biodiverse World is a place for collaborating with others to develop technological solutions for critical conservation problems facing our world.

Participants with background, experience, or interest in any field relevant to conservation are needed. For example: Biology, Zoology, Ecology, Environmental Science, Wildlife Management, etc. Designers, Artists, and Photographers are also needed.
